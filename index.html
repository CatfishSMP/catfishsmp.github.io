How to Join

Bedrock (Mobile/PC)

  1. Open Minecraft and click Play.
  2. Go to the Servers tab.
  3. Scroll down and click Add Server.
  4. Enter Catfish SMP as the name.
  5. Enter the IP and Port shown above.
  6. Click Save & Play!

Java Edition (PC)

  1. Open Minecraft and click Multiplayer.
  2. Click Add Server or Direct Connection.
  3. Enter Catfish SMP as the name.
  4. Paste the Recommended IP into the Server Address box.
  5. Click Done and join the server!
